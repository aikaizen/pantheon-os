Portal Model

← Pantheon OS

◆Concept

The portal is the shared membrane between harnesses and between operating surfaces. It is the connective tissue that preserves context, state, and continuity as work moves across agents, sessions, and surfaces.

◆Contains

Task state
Summaries
Checkpoints
Artifacts
Handoff notes
Approvals
Escalation markers
Logs
Operational context

◆Why It Matters

Without the portal, agent work is trapped in local sessions and raw output. The portal makes work portable — a task can move from one agent to another, from one harness to another, from one surface to another, without losing context.
