# Company Dashboard Spec

## Purpose
The Company Dashboard is the strategic surface of Pantheon OS.

Its job is to make the company legible.

## Questions it should answer
- What products, prototypes, experiments, and internal systems exist?
- What is active right now?
- What is healthy or unhealthy?
- What needs attention?
- What is being approved, shipped, or blocked?
- Which initiatives are becoming products?

## Core sections
- Portfolio registry
- Activity
- Goals
- Heartbeats
- Approvals
- Budget visibility
- Operators

## Product stance
The dashboard is the most visible current surface of Pantheon OS, but it is not the whole product.
